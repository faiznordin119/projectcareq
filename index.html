Background

Why this app exists

The Malaysian healthcare system is strained by a familiar problem — patients and clinics are both losing time to an outdated process.

The context

A system under pressure

Access to healthcare in both government and private clinics often comes with a familiar frustration: long queues, extended waiting times, and the uncertainty of not knowing how crowded a clinic will be on any given day.

These challenges not only strain patients but also affect clinic staff, who must juggle unpredictable walk-in volumes and heavy administrative workloads. The result is a system where everyone loses — patients spend hours waiting, and doctors see rushed consultations.

CareQ is a proposal to close that gap. It is designed not just as a digital queue system, but as a complete healthcare companion — one that offers convenience, promotes preventive care, improves transparency, and helps patients manage their health with confidence.

Patient journey

How it works, step by step

From the moment you feel unwell to leaving the clinic — CareQ removes every friction point along the way.

Feel unwell at home

Open the app and use the AI symptom checker. Answer a few guided questions to find out whether to rest at home or visit a clinic.

Find the right clinic

The clinic locator shows nearby options with live crowd levels, opening hours, services offered, and estimated consultation costs.

Book your timeslot

Reserve a slot in advance. No more walk-in guesswork — you arrive knowing exactly when you'll be seen.

Arrive and consult

Your health record is already on the app. The doctor gets a clearer picture faster — making the consultation quicker and more effective.

Ongoing self-monitoring

After your visit, continue tracking your health readings. Get medication reminders and lifestyle nudges to stay ahead of your health.

All features

Everything in one place

Eight core features designed to give patients full control and reduce unnecessary pressure on clinics.

1

Appointment booking

Book timeslots at government or private clinics in advance. No more half-day walk-ins or uncertainty about availability.

2

Real-time queue tracker

See live wait times and current patient count before leaving home. Plan your visit around your schedule, not the clinic's.

3

Health metrics log

Track blood pressure, heart rate, body temperature, and glucose levels. The app shows trends over time and alerts you when readings fall outside healthy ranges.

4

AI symptom checker

A guided questionnaire that helps you decide: rest at home, monitor your symptoms, or visit a doctor. Not a diagnostic tool — a decision support tool.

5

Clinic locator

Find the nearest clinic based on your location. Filter by opening hours, services offered, crowd level, and type of facility. Navigate directly via integrated maps.

6

Estimated costs

Know the approximate consultation fees and potential test costs before you arrive. Reduces financial uncertainty, especially for private clinic visits.

7

Digital health record

Securely store medications, past diagnoses, vaccination history, and test results. Share with your doctor in one tap. Includes reminders for medication refills.

8

Lifestyle guidance

Personalised daily calorie goals, balanced meal suggestions, hydration reminders, and exercise nudges based on your age, weight, and activity level.
